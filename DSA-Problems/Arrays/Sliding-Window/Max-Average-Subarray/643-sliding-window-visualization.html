ℹ️ Strategy
Phase 1: Sum the first k elements. That's the initial window.
Phase 2: For each i from k to n−1: sum += nums[i] (entering) and sum −= nums[i−k] (leaving).
Track max. Divide by k at the end. O(1) per slide.
