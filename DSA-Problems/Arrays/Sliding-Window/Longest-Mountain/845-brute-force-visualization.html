Brute Force: Iterate Center & Expand
Time: O(N²) | Space: O(1)

ℹ️ Logic
1. Iterate i from 1 to N-2.
2. Check if arr[i] is a Peak (greater than neighbors).
3. If Peak, expand Left then Right to find boundaries.
4. Calculate length: R - L + 1.
