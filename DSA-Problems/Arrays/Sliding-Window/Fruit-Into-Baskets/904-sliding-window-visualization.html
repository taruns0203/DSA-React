ℹ️ Strategy
Maintain a window [left, right] and a frequency map.
Expand right each step, adding the fruit to the map.
If map.size > 2, shrink from left until ≤ 2 types remain.
After each valid state, update maxPicked = max(maxPicked, right − left + 1).
